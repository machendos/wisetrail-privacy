Privacy Policy for Wisetrail

Last updated: July 2026

Wisetrail is a personal finance and spending tracking app that helps users add transactions, review spending, manage recurring payments, view analytics, set spending goals, and understand their budget.

Information We Collect

Wisetrail may collect information that you manually enter into the app, including transactions, categories, recurring payments, budgets, spending goals, and related financial notes.

The app may also collect basic account information, such as your email address, if account creation, login, backup, or sync functionality is enabled.

How We Use Information

We use your information only to provide app functionality, including saving your transactions, showing analytics, calculating recurring payment impact, tracking budget progress, and syncing your data if applicable.

Data Storage

Your data may be stored locally on your device and/or on our backend servers if cloud sync, account access, or backup functionality is used.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your spending data with advertisers.

Third-Party Services

Wisetrail may use third-party services for app infrastructure, authentication, analytics, crash reporting, hosting, or other functionality required to operate the app. These services may process limited technical information needed to provide their services.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account or personal data by contacting us at the email address below.
